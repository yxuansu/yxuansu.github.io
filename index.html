I am a third-year (final-year) Ph.D. student at Language Technology Lab, University of Cambridge, advised by Prof. Nigel Collier. Previously, I recieved my M.Phil in Machine Learning, Speech and Language Technology (MLSALT) from Engineering Department of University of Cambridge. And I recieved my Bachelor degree from Engineering Department of Beijing Institute of Technology.

I am broadly interested in natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning. Previously, I mainly investigated how to augment generative models with explicit memories (either internal or external) and their applications in paraphrasing (ACL2021), data-to-text generation (EMNLP2021, EMNLP2021), and dialogue systems (TASLP, ACL2021). More recently, I have shifted my research interest to non-autoregressive generation models (EACL2021) as well as representation learning.
