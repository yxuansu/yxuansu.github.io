I am a final-year Ph.D. student at Language Technology Lab, University of Cambridge, advised by Prof. Nigel Collier. Previously, I recieved my M.Phil in Machine Learning, Speech and Language Technology (MLSALT) from Engineering Department of University of Cambridge. And I obtained my Bachelor degree from Engineering Department of Beijing Institute of Technology.

I am broadly interested in natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning. Some of my research areas include prototype-augmented text generation [1][2][3][4], non-autoregressive text generation [5], and task-oriented dialogue systems [6]. Recently, I focus my research on the topic of contrastive learning and the study of its potential in language model pre-training [8], discourse representation learning [9][10], knowledge probing [11], open-ended text generation [12], and plug-and-play multi-modal text generation [13].
